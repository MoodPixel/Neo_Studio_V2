# Relationship

## Identity

Use this record for bonds, rivalries, romantic partnerships, familial ties, political alliances, and other runtime relationship states.

## Participants

Define the character IDs involved. Character A and Character B are used for the primary dyad; extra participants can be added for triads, found-family groups, or factional dynamics.

## Runtime use

Relationship records compile into relationship_state memory, relationship graph edges, and rp_relationship_state rows. Scene Chat should use these records to preserve emotional logic, boundaries, conflict patterns, and unresolved tension.
